Project: Multi-Dialect Opera Synthesis Addressing the Cultural Needs of New Generation Immigrants

Overview: This demo illustrates our model's capability to preserve traditional vocal characteristics while adapting to personalized user inputs.

Feature Highlight: Dialect Mixing (e.g., Cantonese + Mandarin) caters to immigrant communities who retain specific dialect phrases ("Code-Switching") within standard artistic expression.
